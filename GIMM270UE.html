GIMM 270 Unreachable Eviorment (FALL 2025)

For this project I had to make an "Unreachable Place" in Unreal. So for this project I decided to go with a location in a game I plan on making called "The Dimond Sea""". The basic idaho behinde the location is that it is a magical lake that is of similar size to The Salt Lake in Utah and The Great Lakes. I spent much of my time foucusing on the atmosphere and the natural part of the level as I had deemed them to be the main parts of the scene, imparticularly giving the dimond sea a blueish glow. This project was a great way for me to hone my skills at level design and really allowed me to strech my creative muscles and also ticker and mess around with audio design.

This was the first project in school where I got the chance to play around with audio design inside of a game engine. I found a few free audio assets and implimented them to the level and made a custom one for a saloon that was in the town scene. To make the custom sound effect, I grabed some plates, dishish, glasses, and silvierware and recorded clinking them together.

Overall I learned a lot from this project about level design in the unreal engine and made something which I am proud of. I also got the chance to play around with audio design which is something I was really excited to get to do.
